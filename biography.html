Rohit Sharma - Biography

Career

Rohit Sharma is an Indian cricketer who is widely regarded as one of the finest batsmen in the modern era. He has represented the Indian national cricket team in all three formats of the game.

ODI Debut: 23 June 2007

T20I Debut: 19 September 2007

Test Debut: 6 November 2013

Captaincy

Rohit Sharma has also showcased his leadership skills by captaining the Indian cricket team in various tournaments and series. Notably, he has been the captain of the Indian team in limited-overs formats, including leading the team to victory in the 2018 Asia Cup and the 2021 T20 World Cup.

Records

Rohit Sharma holds several records in international cricket, including:

  • Highest individual score in ODIs - 264 runs
  • Most double centuries in ODIs - 3
  • Fastest century in T20Is - 35 balls

Contributions and Achievements

Rohit Sharma has made significant contributions to the Indian cricket team's success over the years. He has played crucial innings in important matches and has been a key factor in India's victories in various tournaments, including multiple ICC events.

Some of his notable achievements include:

  • Multiple IPL titles with the Mumbai Indians
  • Leading run-scorer in the 2019 ODI World Cup
  • ICC Cricketer of the Year in 2019
